Unit 1

Unit 1 educated me on the purpose of research; research is twofold for any student, regardless of the level they are pursuing as it can help improve critical thinking, understanding of a particular topic and most importantly, make evidence-based decisions. Research is classified into three purposes; exploration, description and explanation (Singh, 2019). The use of the scientific method for research is classified in two categories; deductive and inductive with the major difference being the order of the process. Deductive reasoning starts with the theory, developing a hypothesis, collecting and analyzing data to test the hypothesis, whereas inductive reasoning starts with collecting and analyzing data, and stating the hypothesis (Decarlo, 2018).
